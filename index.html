ODYSSEY GUIDE

Basic Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys: Move your Runeweaver around the battlefield.

Mouse Cursor: Aim spells.

Left Click (LMB): Cast Primary Spell.

Right Click (RMB): Cast Secondary Spell.

Spacebar: Cast Utility / Dash Spell.

Q / E Key: Cast Ultimate Spell (unlocked in ability tree).

Esc / P Key: Pause game.

Elemental Synergies & Combos

Weaving multiple spell types on the same target triggers devastating reaction effects:

Elemental Combinations

  • Fire + Frost = Superheated Steam: Creates an scalding steam cloud dealing high fire DoT.
  • Lightning + Frost = Superconductive Freeze: Deals instant lightning damage and freezes all nearby enemies.
  • Void + Fire = Supernova Singularity: Causes a pulled-in black hole to ignite and detonate in a massive screen-clearing explosion.
  • Time + Lightning = Temporal Overload: Speeds up your cooldowns and grants lightning velocity.

Core Mechanics

  • Ability Tree: Research Cryomancy or Electromancy branches to unlock nodes giving Max Mana and permanent regeneration.
  • Runic Shop: Purchase Mana Potions or the permanent Mana Well stats between waves using shards!

The Aether Web (Ability Tree)

Defeat monsters to collect Aether Shards and gain XP. Leveling up grants Aether Points (AP). Press the ⚡ Tree button at the top-right to open the Ability Tree and customize your build with over 100 upgrades!
